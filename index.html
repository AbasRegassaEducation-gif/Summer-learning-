How to Play
1

Sign in once

Type your name and pick your grade. Your stars are saved so you can come back any time.

2

Play the Weekly Challenge

Each week has 5 math and 5 reading questions at your grade level. Weeks unlock in order — finish one week (and warm up in Math Facts) to open the next, all the way to Week 10.

3

Earn points for everything

Each weekly question is worth 10 points. In the Math Facts Arcade, single-digit facts are 1 point each and the double-digit Bonus Round is 2 points each. It all adds into one total score.

⚠

One try per question — no redos!

You get one chance on each question. Once you answer, it's locked forever. Read carefully and choose your best answer!

🔓

Unlock the next week

A new week opens once you've finished the week before it and spent at least 2 minutes in the Math Facts Arcade. Keep practicing to keep climbing!

⚡

Math Facts Arcade — everyone plays!

Quick Facts (single digits, 5 seconds) earn 1 point each and are open to all grades. Grades 3–8 also unlock the Bonus Round: double digits, 15 seconds, 2 points each. These points count toward your total and help unlock new weeks.

🏆

Climb the leaderboard

Your weekly points and Math Facts points combine into one Total Points leaderboard. The more you play, the higher you climb!

🎁

Win real prizes!

The Weekly Top 3 on the leaderboard each win a free prize box at Bultum — soccer balls, treats, and more. And the Winner of the Month — the student at the top of the Total Points leaderboard — earns a FREE Mall of America ticket!
